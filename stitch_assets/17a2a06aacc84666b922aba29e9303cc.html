Problem Space: Complexity

The game of Connect 4 possesses a state-space complexity of approximately 4.5 × 10¹³ legal positions. For a standard 6x7 board, the number of possible game paths is significantly larger, necessitating an optimized search strategy to achieve real-time decision-making.
